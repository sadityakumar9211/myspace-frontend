MySpace
A socio-academic portal for Students

MySpace is a socio-academic site for the students of ABV-IIITM Gwalior. Students can access and download a ton of helpful resources including but not limited to notes, previous year papers, etc. They can ask their doubts and share their ideas and knowledge through the QA and Article Section. They can also check their class schedule and upcoming college events.
